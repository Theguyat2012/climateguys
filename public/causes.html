The Causes of Global Warming

Humans are believed to be one of the most influential cuases of global warming. While humans expand the greenhouse effect become more and more relevent. This is a phenomenon in which the sun's heat is trapped within the lower areas in the atmosphere Greenhouse gases that are produced here at home traps heat and releases that same heat into space.
