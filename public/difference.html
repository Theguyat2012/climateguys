The Differences Between Climate Change and Global Warming

Global Warming

Global Warming is an influence of climate change. This term is used to describe the increase of the world's average temperatures. It focuses on sudden and unatural changes in the world's environment. Global Warming affects the entire Earth.

Climate Change

Climate Change is the long term change in in the Earth of region's typical weather.
