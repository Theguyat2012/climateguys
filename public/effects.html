Detrimental Effects of Global Warming

Earth

The sea level has risen 8 inches since 1880 and is expected to increase up to 1-4 feet by 2100. The average global temperature has risen 1.1 - 1.6o Fahrenheit since 1906. The amount of CO2 is being produced faster than it can be used. Additionally, the climate changes, ecosystems are destroyed, species become extinct. We only have one Earth, take care of it.

North America

North America has been at risk of increased flooding, heatwaves, heatwave intensity, wildfire, insect outbreaks, and "mood swings" in weather.

South America

Peoplpe had been displaced, lost, killed, and the civilizations suffered servere economic lost. Brazil had its first major drought since 80 years. Europe

Europe is extremely vulnerable. Water surrounds the thin continent of Europe, also sea levels are rising. Temperatures in Europe are 1.3oC higher than the per-industrial age average. Heatwaves became longer and hotter. With droughts, agriculture becomes more difficult.

Africa

Africa is being hit with more droughts and increased temperatures as if the land wasn't hot and dry enough, even rivers evaporated. The coastlines in Africa costed up to $400 million after being hit with flooding. The Kilimanjaro mountain was once covered in ice and snow. Now,most of it is gone.

Asia

Asia will arguably be the most affected by global warm with so many renown cities and countries built by the coastline (i.e. Seoul, Singspore, Saigon, Tokyo, South Korea, Japan). East Asian Countries are susceptible to constant flooding merely by being tropical in addition to Global Warming. The land of East Asian Countries are mainly used for agriculture. With flooding, famine is likely to occur.

Arctic and Antarctica

The entire land mass is melting. The Arctic and Antarctica are slowly decaying. The home to unique organisms is fading.
